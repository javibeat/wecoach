Corporate Wellbeing Program

Thrive360 is a year-long program specifically designed to enhance employee wellbeing in the workplace while at the same time enhancing your company branding; a people-centered working environment will position your organization as a pioneer in the industry as well as an employer of choice.

Through tailored workshops and individual support we aim to achieve the following for your organization:

Higher Productivity: Mentally and physically healthy employees have more levels of energy and focus. Less absenteeism and stress contribute to consistent productivity. Motivated Workforce: Keeping employees engaged in professional and personal development activities foster a sense of progress and achievement, enhancing job satisfaction and motivation. Integrated Workplace: Embedding wellness into the company culture shows a shared commitment to health and work-life balance, creating a supportive and unified work environment.

Our wellness program is structured around the 6 pillars of wellbeing:

Physical:

Maintaining physical health through regular exercise, proper nutrition, and adequate rest.

Mental:

The ability to cope with life’s challenges and work fruitfully to thrive in all aspects.

Emotional:

Being able to understand, accept, and express our emotions in a healthy and constructive manner.

Financial:

Developing financial resilience by budgeting, saving, and planning a healthy financial future.

Professional:

Finding purpose, fulfillment, and a sense of accomplishment in your career.

Social:

Foster and maintain meaningful connections, and contribute to a supportive community.
